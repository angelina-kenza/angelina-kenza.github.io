Kenza Group

Kenza Group launched in June 2018 with one simple mission: "Deliver successful business change to it's key clients." We are passionate about changing the way businesses think, and operate. Founded by Angela Kakiiza the company has set out to build it's brand based on integrity, and relationships and not simply profits. We view our clients as partners whom we work with in close collaboration on a one-to-one basis, as together we grow on our journey to success.

Whatever your challenge, whether it be digital, or entering into a new market, we break the problem down to manageable chunks and together we drive change taking an agile approach.
